1
Marketing Period

During this phase, we'll showcase your property and gauge market interest without revealing your price expectations. Premium prices are typically achieved within the first 30 days, and our deadline strategy addresses concerns about extended market time while encouraging buyers to act decisively.

We are essentially casting a net (rather than casting a single line) and capturing as much enquiry and inspections as possible. This allows us to identify and interact with more buyers which results in more competition which drives price.

Key Outcomes:

  • Achieve a sale within the first 30 days to capitalize on the prime selling window
  • Assess market interest and identify key committed buyers
  • Create urgency through deadlines that prompt buyers to act
  • Pre-defined timeline keeps the selling plan crystal clear
2
Pre-Auction Sale/Auction

Once we've identified our key buyers, we'll strategize whether to proceed with an auction or sell prior. Currently we've been selling roughly 4/10 homes prior to auction, however renovated homes are seeing more competition among buyers, so having the auction ready and available to leverage is a real advantage.

Our Strategy:

‚Ä¢ Run to auction if multiple buyers are offering similar prices, leveraging the transparent and competitive environment to maximize your return.

‚Ä¢ Sell prior to auction if a single buyer stands out with a compelling, unconditional offer that meets your goals.

3
Post-Auction Strategy

After the auction, we shift to our post-auction offers closing strategy, negotiating with existing buyers to secure the best possible offer. If the property passes in, don't worry‚Äîit's not a fail. It's just the vendors positioning themselves to negotiate a higher price.
